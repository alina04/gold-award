My Motivation


The reason I chose this specific topic and this project was because of my sister. I was 12 at the time when she left home and began college. Now, I am in her position. However, little did I know then, that she was battling depression and anxiety, but went through it alone. At 12 years old, I didn’t know why she was feeling the way she was or how to help her. I could only watch her suffer as I stood by. Since then, she has fallen but has always managed to pick herself up and put herself back together. Now, I’m 17 and I’ve learned about her hardships and how she battled it all herself. This is a feeling I’ll never understand but it was something that hurt me to watch as I grew up. I want to share with the world that mental health issues shouldn’t be ignored and no one should have to fight alone. My sister has always been my inspiration because of how she fought alone yet became the amazing person she is today. Today, she is the one person I can always turn to and the one person I can always ask for advice. Not only has she grown as a person but she opened my eyes to how terribly mental health issues can take a toll on your life.
